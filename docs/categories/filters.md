# Filters

The filters category focuses on image-domain operations that preserve a retoucher's core signal: texture, edges, and controllable smoothness. These are the pack's foundational cleanup nodes.

- **N-01 GPU Frequency Separation** — Splits an image into low-frequency color/form and high-frequency detail.
- **N-03 Edge-Aware Skin Smoother** — Applies bilateral smoothing with mask-aware blending and device controls.

Lean-batch flagship pages: [N-29 Alpha Matte Extractor](../nodes/n29-alpha-matte-extractor.md), [N-33 Mask Edge Smoother](../nodes/n33-mask-edge-smoother.md), and [N-10 Face Detect](../nodes/n10-face-detect.md).
